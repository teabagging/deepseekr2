❓Edward earns money by mowing lawns and cleaning gardens. Lawns are categorized into small, medium, and large, with payment rates of 8 dollars, 12 dollars, and 15 dollars respectively. He mowed 3 small lawns, 1 medium lawn, and 1 large lawn, and cleaned a total of 5 gardens. He had expenses of 10 dollars for fuel and 15 dollars for equipment rental, but he already had 7 dollars saved up.
